System Architecture
Built for contested environments.
A zero-trust, edge-native architecture designed to operate when every other system has failed. No cloud dependency. No single point of failure.
  • DDIL-First Design — Full autonomy in Denied, Disrupted, Intermittent, and Limited connectivity environments. Zero reliance on external compute.
  • Hardware Agnostic — Deploys across Jetson Orin, AMD Xilinx Versal, Intel Arc, and ruggedized x86 with unified model format (ONNX Runtime).
  • MIL-STD-810H Qualified — Tested across full environmental envelope: -40°C to +71°C operating range, 40G mechanical shock, salt fog, sand, and dust.
  • Zero-Trust Mesh — End-to-end encrypted sensor-to-display pipeline. Mutual TLS between all nodes. Air-gapped model update pathway.
  • Open Architecture — CMOSS/SOSA-aligned. Sensor-agnostic MISB ST 0601/0903 metadata compliance. TAK-compatible CoT output.
